Michel's website is a perfect example of how to use a website as a portfolio. He has very simple navigation buttons which are just a series of arrows that lead you through his work. He is also very consistent in color and font. He has all his project separated into categories which makes it easy to find what your looking for. The cut off text of those categories does bother me a bit because at the maximum screen size they are not cut off so I feel like he should have accounted for different screen sizes. I think its nice that has has minimal text on his site, I like the big icons that he used as a way of separating his work. I think the text explaining that each category is can be lost because it takes away from the icons.
