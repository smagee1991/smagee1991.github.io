I saw they used a lot of maps which immediatly cought my attention because thats a feature I’m using Im my app. It was really helpful for me to see the way another app laid things out. This app is also very simple and easy too use which is something you have to keep in mind when designing your app. Comparing it to my app I think mine has more features and while they both pretain to different things they still both have a location feature, a sharing with friends feature and a favorites freature. I will definatly take some ideas from Rego.
