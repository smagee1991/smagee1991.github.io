I am currently a student at Mica (The Maryland Institute College of Art) studying graphic design and photography and before Mica I attended Carroll Community College. I feel like graphic design and photography go hand and hand and really enjoy seeing how they overlap. I design things from logos to websites to T-shirts. I also do portrait photography which I love. The goal with my work (photography in particular) is to challenge the viewer to see things differently and for them to relate to the messages I try to show throughout a seris. I typically work in black and white whether it be photography or graphic design. I like my work to be clean yet captivating and black and white has a classy feel that gets that across.
